WHERE
Venue

The ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception will all be held at Poets Cove Resort & Spa on Pender Island BC.

9801 Spalding Road
Pender Island, BC V0N 2M3

Travel

Ferries run between Victoria or Vancouver and Pender Island. Schedules can be found on the BC Ferries website. Another option for travelling to Pender is float planes which can take you directly to Poets Cove's dock. Kenmore Air and Harbour Air are float plane companies in the area. You can book a flight on their website.

Transit

On the day of the wedding we will have shuttle service to drive people with accomodations outside of Poets Cove. For transportation before or after the wedding day you can call Pender Island Taxi Co.
