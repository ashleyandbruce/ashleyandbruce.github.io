Where to Stay

The island has the two main resorts Poets Cove and WOODS. Both of these resorts have hotel rooms, cabins, and alternatives accomodations. Campsite are available at Port Browning Marina. The island also has several Bed and Breakfasts some of which are run through Airbnb.

Ferry

One option for getting to Pender Island is ferries. You can catch a ferry on either Victoria or Vancouver.

For schedules visit the BC ferries website.

WEBSITE

Plane

Another option for travelling to Pender is float planes which can take you directly to Poets Cove's dock. Kenmore Air and Harbour Air are float plane companies in the area. You can book a flight on their websites.

Taxi

On the day of the wedding we will have shuttle service to drive people with accomodations outside of Poets Cove. For transportation before or after the wedding day you can call Pender Island Taxi Co.
