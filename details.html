Schedule
CEREMONY

4PM - 5PM

The ceremony will be held on the Sea Glass Terrace at the resort. This is located in front of the main hotel overlooking the beach and marina.

COCKTAIL HOUR

5PM - 6PM

While the bride, groom, family, and wedding party are taking photos all other guests can enjoy punch and appetizers in the Sea Glass Ballroom. This is located inside the hotel on the main level. It is right behind the Sea Glass Terrace where the reception will be held.

RECEPTION

6PM - 12AM

The reception will be held in the Sea Glass Ballroom as well. Look at the cocktail hour description for details on it's location. We will start with some appetizers and wine at the tables. Then the dinner BBQ will begin. You will have the choice of steak, chicken, or salmon.
