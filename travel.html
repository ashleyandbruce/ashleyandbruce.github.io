Getting To Pender

BC Ferries

Ferries go to Pender Island from Victoria and Vancouver.
For schedule information go to their website.

Website

Kenmore Air

Kenmore Air flies seaplanes from Seattle to Poets Cove.
For more information or to book a flight go to their website.

Water Taxi

Water taxis can take you from Sidney to Pender Island.
