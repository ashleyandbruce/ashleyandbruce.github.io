Our Story

“You come to love not by finding the perfect person, but by seeing an imperfect person perfectly.”

Sam Keen

It all began in Computer Science 231 among a sea of 0's and 1's. Ashley and Bruce were both in their first year of Computer Science. Bruce first noticed Ashley sitting in front of him, she stuck out in his mind because of her long brown, curly hair and the irregular way she sat - the only one in the room with her legs crossed sitting in her chair. They crossed paths again at a party hosted by a mutual friend and quickly became good friends. The following summer Ashley invited Bruce on a camping trip in Banff National Park. Sparks flew over a bonfire as they roasted marshmallows and hotdogs, they have been inseparable ever since.
