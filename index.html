OUR STORY
HOW WE MET
It all began in Computer Science 231 among a sea of 0's and 1's. We were both in our first year of Computer Science and had enrolled in the same 231 lecture. We were introduced to eachother by a mutual friend. We crossed paths again at a party and quickly became good friends. That summer we went on a camping trip to Banff with friends. Not long after we started dating.
HOW HE ASKED
Bruce proposed on July 23rd 2018 in Banff. We had made plans to go to Banff for the weekend. When we got there Bruce suggested going to Lake Minniwanka which is the lake we had visited on that first camping trip together. When we arrived he got down on one knee and proposed. Afterwards we had a really nice fondue dinner with champagne!
